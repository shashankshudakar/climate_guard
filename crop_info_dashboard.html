Seasonal Focus

Typical farming periods in Karnataka:

  • Kharif (Monsoon): June to October. Major crops: Rice, Maize, Cotton, Ragi.
  • Rabi (Winter): October to March. Major crops: Wheat, Jowar, Bengal Gram.
  • Summer: March to June. Irrigated crops.

Note: Rainfall is crucial during the Kharif season.
