Peak Fire-Risk Window

Typical vigilance period in dry forest belts:

  • February to May: leaf litter ignition risk rises.
  • Afternoon watch: heat and low humidity increase spread.
  • Buffer belts: remove dry biomass near forest edges.

Note: Community patrols and early alerts reduce damage significantly.
